# AIRGP Steering Committee Charter

## 1. Purpose

The AIRGP Steering Committee governs the evolution of the Artificial Intelligence Runtime Governance Protocol specification, manages the conformance program, and oversees community engagement.

## 2. Steward

ThinkNEO AI Technology Company Limited (Hong Kong) is the founding steward of AIRGP. The steward holds copyright over the specification text and trademark rights over "AIRGP".

## 3. Membership Tiers

### 3.1 Founding Member

- **Organization:** ThinkNEO AI Technology Company Limited
- **Rights:** Permanent seat, veto on specification changes, trademark governance
- **Obligations:** Maintain reference implementation, host specification infrastructure

### 3.2 Promoter Members

- **Eligibility:** Invited by the Steering Committee; organizations with significant AI governance deployments or complementary specifications
- **Rights:** Voting rights on specification amendments, early access to draft revisions, logo usage
- **Obligations:** Maintain at least one conformant implementation, participate in quarterly reviews
- **Seats:** Up to 12

### 3.3 Adopter Members

- **Eligibility:** Any organization that signs the AIRGP Adopter Agreement
- **Rights:** Participate in working groups, submit specification feedback, use conformance badge (upon passing)
- **Obligations:** Adhere to the AIRGP Trademark Usage Policy
- **Seats:** Unlimited

## 4. Decision Making

- **Specification amendments:** Require 2/3 majority of Promoter votes plus Founding Member consent
- **Conformance program changes:** Require simple majority of Promoter votes
- **Administrative matters:** Decided by the Founding Member

## 5. Specification Amendment Process

1. Proposal submitted as a GitHub Issue with the `spec-amendment` label
2. 30-day public comment period
3. Working group review and revision
4. Steering Committee vote
5. If approved, incorporated into the next minor or major version

## 6. Intellectual Property Policy

Modeled on the W3C Patent Policy. All members grant a royalty-free, non-exclusive license to any essential claims in their contributions to the AIRGP specification. See `governance/ipr-policy.md` for details.

## 7. Meetings

- Quarterly steering committee meetings (virtual)
- Annual in-person summit (location rotates)
- Working group meetings as needed

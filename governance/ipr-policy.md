# AIRGP Intellectual Property Rights Policy

Modeled on the W3C Patent Policy.

## 1. Licensing Obligation

Each Steering Committee member grants to all AIRGP implementers a royalty-free, non-exclusive, worldwide license under any Essential Claims in contributions they make to the AIRGP specification.

## 2. Essential Claims

"Essential Claims" means patent claims that would necessarily be infringed by a conformant implementation of a normative portion of the AIRGP specification.

## 3. Exclusion Procedure

Members may exclude specific patents from the licensing obligation within 150 days of a specification draft being published for review, by providing written notice to the Steering Committee.

## 4. Defensive Termination

The royalty-free license granted under this policy terminates automatically for any party that files a patent infringement claim against an AIRGP-conformant implementation based on the specification.

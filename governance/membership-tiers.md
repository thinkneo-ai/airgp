# AIRGP Membership Tiers

## Founding Member

- **Organization:** ThinkNEO AI Technology Company Limited
- **Rights:** Permanent Committee seat, veto on spec changes, trademark governance, reference implementation stewardship
- **Obligations:** Maintain spec infrastructure, host conformance program, provide reference implementation
- **Fee:** N/A

## Promoter Member

- **Eligibility:** Invitation by Steering Committee; organizations with production AI governance deployments
- **Rights:** Vote on spec amendments, early draft access, conformance badge, listed on website
- **Obligations:** Maintain conformant implementation, quarterly meeting attendance, annual renewal
- **Fee:** To be determined by Steering Committee
- **Seats:** Up to 12

## Adopter Member

- **Eligibility:** Any organization signing the Adopter Agreement
- **Rights:** Working group participation, feedback submission, conformance badge (upon passing)
- **Obligations:** Comply with Trademark Usage Policy
- **Fee:** Free
- **Seats:** Unlimited

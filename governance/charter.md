# AIRGP Steering Committee Charter

## Article 1: Establishment

The AIRGP Steering Committee is established to govern the evolution, maintenance, and adoption of the Artificial Intelligence Runtime Governance Protocol.

## Article 2: Composition

The Committee consists of members from three tiers as defined in `membership-tiers.md`. The Founding Member (ThinkNEO) holds a permanent seat.

## Article 3: Responsibilities

1. Approve specification amendments
2. Manage the conformance program
3. Resolve disputes regarding trademark usage
4. Coordinate with related standards bodies
5. Promote adoption of AIRGP

## Article 4: Meetings

Quarterly virtual meetings. Annual in-person summit. Emergency sessions as needed with 72-hour notice.

## Article 5: Voting

See `../GOVERNANCE.md` for voting procedures per membership tier.

## Article 6: Amendments

This charter may be amended by unanimous Promoter vote plus Founding Member consent.
